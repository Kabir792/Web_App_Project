# **Non-Functional Requirements**

## **Performance**

* The system shall respond within 3 seconds under normal load.  
* The system shall support multiple concurrent users.

## **Security**

* Passwords shall be stored securely.  
* Unauthorized access shall be prevented.  
* User sessions shall be protected.

## **Reliability**

* The system shall maintain data consistency.  
* The system shall minimize downtime.

## **Usability**

* The interface shall be easy to use.  
* Navigation shall be simple and intuitive.

## **Availability**

* The system shall be accessible through web browsers.  
* The system shall be available 24/7.

## **Maintainability**

* The codebase shall be modular and easy to maintain.  
* Documentation shall be provided.

## **Scalability**

* The system shall support future feature additions.  
* The database shall handle increasing numbers of students.
