# **Interviews**

## **Interview 1: Administrator**

### **Questions**

1. What difficulties do you face managing student records?  
2. What features would improve your workflow?

### **Responses**

* Manual updates are time-consuming.  
* Searching for student records is difficult.  
* Automated reports would be beneficial.

## **Interview 2: Student**

### **Questions**

1. What information would you like to access online?  
2. What challenges do you face obtaining academic information?

### **Responses**

* Grades and attendance should be easily accessible.  
* Notifications would be useful.
